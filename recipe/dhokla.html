DHOKLA

Description

Dhokla is a popular Gujarati snack known for its fluffy texture and tangy flavor. It is made from fermented batter primarily consisting of gram flour (besan) and flavored with a blend of spices. Dhokla is steamed and often tempered with mustard seeds and curry leaves for added aroma and taste.

Ingredients

  • Gram flour (besan)
  • Yogurt
  • Water
  • Lemon juice
  • Fruit salt (eno)
  • Spices (such as turmeric, green chili, ginger, and salt)
  • Mustard seeds (for tempering)
  • Curry leaves (for tempering)

How to Cook

  1. Mix besan (gram flour) with yogurt, water, and spices.
  2. Add lemon juice and fruit salt to the batter, and mix well.
  3. Steam the batter in a greased pan until cooked, and temper with mustard seeds and curry leaves.
