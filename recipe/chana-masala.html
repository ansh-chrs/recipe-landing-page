CHANA MASALA

DESCRIPTION

Chana Masala is a flavorful Indian dish made with chickpeas cooked in a spiced tomato-based gravy, typically featuring aromatic spices like cumin, coriander, turmeric, and garam masala.

Ingredients

  • Chickpeas
  • Tomatoes
  • Cumin
  • Coriander
  • Turmeric
  • Garam Masala
