Samosa

Description

Samosas are a popular Indian snack. They are made with a crispy outer covering of flour dough and a savory filling of boiled potatoes, peas, onions, and a blend of aromatic spices.

Ingredients

  • Potatoes: Boiled and diced
  • Peas: Cooked and drained
  • Onion: Finely chopped
  • Spices: Garam masala, cumin, coriander, turmeric, and red chili powder
  • Flour: All-purpose or wheat flour
  • Salt: To taste
  • Oil: For frying

How to Cook

  1. Prepare the filling by saut√©ing onions, potatoes, peas, and spices.
  2. Make the dough by mixing flour, salt, and oil, then knead with water.
  3. Divide the dough into small balls and roll each into a thin circle.
  4. Cut the circle in half and fold into a cone shape.
  5. Fill the cone with the prepared filling and seal the edges.
  6. Deep fry the samosas until golden brown and crispy.
  7. Remove from oil and drain excess oil on a paper towel.
  8. Serve hot with chutney or sauce.
